Movement:
Use the arrow keys to move and up to jump.
Right: clockwise
Left: counter-clockwise

Objective:
Avoid the falling meteors for as long as possible on the planet. The white ring around the planet
indicates the lowest point at which meteors can fall from.

-Special Keys-
Main menu: press the space bar (haha) to start the game
Game over screen: press enter to return to main menu
Press 'P' in game to pause
